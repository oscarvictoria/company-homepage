Our Story

Trailmate was started by two college friends—Alex and Sam—after a backpacking trip through South America in 2014. We landed in Lima, then made our way through Cusco, Santiago, and Patagonia. The views were unforgettable. The gear? Not so much. Sam’s zipper broke on the second day, and Alex’s pack felt like carrying a pile of bricks. Every bus ride, every hike, every hostel reminded us how miserable it can be when your backpack just isn’t built for the journey. That trip made one thing crystal clear: the wrong bag can ruin the right adventure. We couldn’t find the backpack we needed. So, we built it. Trailmate’s mission is simple: to make carrying your world feel effortless. Here’s what that means to us.
